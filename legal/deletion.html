Account Deletion Request

Effective Date: 12-Oct-2024

At Tycoon Mart, we value your privacy and understand that you may want to delete your account and associated data. Below are the steps to request account deletion, as well as information about the types of data we store and our retention policy

1. How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  • Send an Email: Compose an email to our support team at support@tycoonmart.com.
  • Include Required Information: In your email, please provide the following details:
    • Your registered email address
    • A clear request for account deletion
  • Confirmation Email: You will receive a confirmation email once your request has been processed.

2. Data Deletion Policy

When you request the deletion of your account, the following data will be deleted:

  • Account Information: All personal details associated with your account (e.g., name, email address, phone number).
  • Transaction History: Records of your purchases and transactions.
  • User Preferences: Any settings or preferences saved in your account.

3. Data Retention

We retain certain types of data for the following periods:

  • Transaction Data: Kept for a period of [insert retention period, e.g., 2 years] for legal and auditing purposes. This data is anonymized and cannot be linked back to your account after deletion.
  • Backup Data: May be retained in our backup systems for a maximum of [insert retention period, e.g., 30 days] before being permanently deleted.

Note: Data that is deleted cannot be recovered. Please ensure you have saved any information you wish to keep before submitting your deletion request.

For any questions or further assistance regarding account deletion, feel free to contact our support team at support@tycoonmart.com.
